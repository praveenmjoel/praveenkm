Built for scale,
designed for trust.

I lead the systems that make sure earners get paid — reliably, on time, across every market Uber operates in. That's the job. Behind it is five years of payments infrastructure, countless PSP integrations, and a deep belief that disbursements is one of the most consequential, and most underrated, problems in fintech.

My path here was unconventional. Civil Engineering at Anna University. Steel Authority of India. An MBA at IIM Ahmedabad. Samsung's Business Innovation team. Leading Paytm's regional operations during demonetisation — one of the most intense periods in Indian payments history. Then Uber, where I spent three years understanding the earner experience from the ground up before pivoting entirely into payments.

I write and speak about disbursements, payout infrastructure, and the future of platform payments. I believe the earner side of money movement — how platforms pay the people who power them — is where the next decade of fintech innovation will be won or lost.
